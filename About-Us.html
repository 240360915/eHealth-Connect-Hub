🏥 Our story

Redefining how
healthcare works

e-Health Connect was built to solve two problems at once — giving unemployed doctors a platform to practice digitally, and giving patients across South Africa access to verified, affordable healthcare from anywhere.

Who we are

A team passionate about
healthcare access

e-Health Connect is a digital healthcare platform with a dual purpose — making healthcare more accessible for patients, while creating real economic opportunities for qualified but unemployed or underutilised doctors across South Africa.

Too many skilled doctors graduate and struggle to find stable employment or build a patient base. At the same time, patients — especially in underserved areas — can't easily find or afford a doctor. We exist to fix both sides of that problem.

Through our platform, doctors can register, get verified, set their own hours and rates, and start seeing patients online immediately — no physical practice required. Patients find a verified doctor, book a slot, and consult via video from wherever they are.

The problem

Why we built this

South Africa's healthcare system faces serious challenges. We set out to tackle the biggest ones head-on.

01
Doctor unemployment

Thousands of qualified doctors in South Africa are unemployed or underemployed. We give them a verified digital practice so they can start earning immediately.

02
Limited healthcare access

Many patients — especially in rural areas — can't easily reach a doctor. Our platform brings verified doctors directly to patients via secure video consultation.

03
No digital infrastructure

Most independent doctors have no way to manage bookings, prescriptions or records online. e-Health Connect gives them a full digital practice out of the box.
